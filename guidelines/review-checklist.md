# Review Checklist

Before finishing a meditation, verify:

- The primary text is clearly identified and remains central.
- The meditation follows a contemplative and ascetical movement rather than a
  merely explanatory or academic one.
- Project-specific metadata and source links are present when required.
- Related meditations in the same project have been considered.
- Required project-specific source families are present and not forced.
- Direct quotations are real, traceable, italicized, and cited immediately.
- Paraphrases and indirect references use `cf.`.
- Non-Latin quotations preserve the required original language and orthography.
- The Marian dimension is organic and not merely appended.
- The Latin has received a final correction pass for agreement, government,
  mood, voice, idiom, and consistency of address.
